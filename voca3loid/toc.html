Summary

The city of Piapro is an eden for actors and is home to many theatre companies along its famous Piapro Street. After receiving a mysterious message hinting at the whereabouts of her missing mother, Miku Hatsune visits Piapro and encounters the director and manager of VOX Company, a theatre company drowning in debt with no audience nor actors.

Solicited by the director to join the company, Miku decides to become an actor to try and help bring it back to its former glory. But first, she needs to find a scriptwriter, as well as scout for more actors to fill out the minimum prerequisite of five actors.
